Christian Youth in Power

Who We Are

Christian Youth in Power (CYP) is the Youth Outreach of the ‘Community of the Good Shepherd.’ The ’CYP,’ is a movement of young people, both college-aged and working youth, who aspire to live as disciples of Our Lord and Saviour, Jesus Christ. We desire to evangelize and disciple the youth and train to be future leaders in the ‘Power of the Holy Spirit’ for the service of the Church and society.
